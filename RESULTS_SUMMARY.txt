## How Robust Kalman Works

**Method:** Constant-velocity motion model + Huber robust loss function

1. **Assumes:** Pedestrian walks with mostly constant velocity (realistic)
2. **Measures:** How much distance per epoch (step)
3. **Detects:** Outliers using Huber loss (quadratic for small, linear for large errors)
4. **Corrects:** Smooths trajectory by downweighting spike measurements
5. **Preserves:** All 393 epochs - no data loss, no artificial gaps

**Why it's better than filtering:**
- ✅ Uses physics (motion model) not just statistics
- ✅ Keeps all epochs (RAIM deletes 52)
- ✅ Doesn't over-smooth like median filter
- ✅ Robust to outliers (unlike standard Kalman)
